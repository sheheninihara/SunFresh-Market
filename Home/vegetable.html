VEGETABLES

Where do we get our Vegetables ?

Farmers' Market

Farmers' markets offer locally grown vegetables, including lettuce, tomatoes, and peppers, with vendors providing samples and cooking tips.

Wholesale Market

Wholesale markets provide supermarkets with bulk fruits and vegetables from farms, ensuring a steady supply without direct customer involvement.

Local Farms or Co-op

Supermarkets partner with local farms for fresh, sustainable vegetables. Customers buy at pick-up points, supporting local agriculture.
